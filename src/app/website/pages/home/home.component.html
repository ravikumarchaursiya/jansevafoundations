Why Janseva Foundation?

Janseva Foundation Sansthan is an organization dedicated to help, support, motivate and guide the Orphan/ Poor Children, Widowed/ divorced or Single Mothers so that they can live a respectful life in a man dominating society. Our main objective is to realize the Orphan/ Poor Children, Widowed/ divorced or Single Mothers that they are not alone rather they have family standing behind them. During Corona and post corona effect many kids / females lost their parents/ husband and forced to live in a miserable life. Even in current society lots of married girls are forced to quit home because of family torture and violence. So objective of SAHYOG Society is to help and support such women to live a respectable life in society.
