During Corona and due to Corona effect, many children became orphans, women lost their husbands and in the present society also many married girls are forced to leave home and live a miserable life due to family oppression and violence. Janseva Foundation is dedicated to helping all such people live a respectable life in the society and making them self-reliant. Janseva Foundation is like a ray of hope for inspiration, guidance and self-reliance for orphans, poor children, poor needy widows and divorced women. The organization believes in the principle that any poor, widow, divorced woman, orphan has the right to live a respectable life in the society. Our main objective is to make orphan, poor, widow, divorced or single mothers realize that they are not alone but Sahayog Sansthan stands behind them like a family. The main objective of Sahyog Foundation will be to provide necessary assistance to the children, widows and women orphaned due to the Corona epidemic and to make them self-reliant by providing appropriate resources. Sahayog Sansthan is dedicated to making a positive impact in the country and society. We aim to address education, employment and self-reliance for orphans, homeless, widows and women through various programs and initiatives. We are able to reach our objective, with the generous support of donors and volunteers. We provide hope and support to needy children and women in times of difficulty. We believe that together we can bring about change and create a brighter future for all.”
